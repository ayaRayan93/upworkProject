Progress support

Tutors and learners can track progress and completion with an array of options for tracking individual activities or resources and at course level.

Media Support

Group activity

Group management to allow group learners to share courses, differentiate activities and facilitate team work.

In-line marking

In-line marking to easily review and provide in-line feedback by annotating files directly within browser

Multi-Language Support

Online Virtual Classroom

Sahih Classrooms Virtual Classroom provides the essential features needed for seamless synchronous and asynchronous collaboration capabilities between presenter (teacher) and attendees (students).

Interactive Whiteboards

Live whiteboard for tutors and groups where annotations are automatically displayed back to the students in real-time. Enabling tutors to share audio, slides, chat, video, and desktop screen with students.

Presenters control

Live Notifications

Send live in-site notifications about product updates and important messages to engage with users precisely when they are using your learning portal.

Session Recording

Playback

Powerful Moderation Tools

Built-in polling

Secure E-mail Invitation
